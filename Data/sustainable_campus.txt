Sustainable Campus Practices

A sustainable campus minimizes environmental impact while promoting efficient resource usage.

Key Practices:
1. Energy Conservation
- Use LED lights.
- Install solar panels.
- Switch off unused equipment.

2. Water Conservation
- Fix leaks promptly.
- Harvest rainwater.
- Reuse water where possible.

3. Waste Management
- Segregate waste.
- Promote recycling.
- Encourage composting.

4. Green Transportation
- Encourage cycling.
- Promote public transport.
- Reduce vehicle emissions.

Benefits:
- Lower operational costs.
- Reduced environmental impact.
- Improved student awareness.
- Contribution to SDGs.
