Waste management helps reduce environmental pollution.

Waste categories:
- Wet waste
- Dry waste
- E-waste
- Hazardous waste

Best practices:
- Segregate waste at source.
- Recycle reusable materials.
- Reduce single-use plastics.
- Promote composting.

Benefits:
- Cleaner campuses.
- Reduced landfill burden.
- Improved public health.
