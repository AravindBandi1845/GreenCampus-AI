Energy Conservation

Energy conservation means reducing energy consumption without affecting productivity or comfort.

Methods of Energy Conservation:
1. Switch off lights and fans when not in use.
2. Use LED lighting.
3. Install energy-efficient appliances.
4. Encourage natural lighting and ventilation.
5. Use renewable energy sources such as solar power.
6. Conduct regular energy audits.

Benefits of Energy Conservation:
- Reduces electricity bills.
- Lowers carbon emissions.
- Conserves natural resources.
- Supports environmental sustainability.

Energy Conservation in Educational Institutions:
- Replace traditional bulbs with LEDs.
- Install solar panels.
- Promote awareness campaigns.
- Use smart energy monitoring systems.
