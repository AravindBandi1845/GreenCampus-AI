Water Conservation

Water conservation refers to the efficient use and management of water resources to reduce wastage and ensure long-term availability.

Methods of Water Conservation:
1. Repair leaking taps and pipelines.
2. Install water-efficient fixtures.
3. Harvest rainwater.
4. Reuse greywater for gardening.
5. Use drip irrigation systems.
6. Reduce unnecessary water consumption.

Importance of Water Conservation:
- Prevents water scarcity.
- Protects ecosystems.
- Ensures sustainable development.
- Reduces water bills.

Water Conservation in Colleges:
- Install rainwater harvesting systems.
- Conduct awareness programs.
- Monitor water usage regularly.
- Promote responsible water consumption among students.
