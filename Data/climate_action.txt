Climate Action

Climate action involves efforts to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and address climate change.

Major Causes of Climate Change:
- Burning fossil fuels.
- Deforestation.
- Industrial emissions.
- Excessive waste generation.

Climate Action Strategies:
1. Increase use of renewable energy.
2. Reduce carbon emissions.
3. Promote sustainable transportation.
4. Plant more trees.
5. Improve waste management practices.

Benefits of Climate Action:
- Cleaner environment.
- Improved public health.
- Reduced global warming.
- Protection of ecosystems.

Role of Students:
- Save energy.
- Reduce plastic usage.
- Participate in environmental campaigns.
- Spread sustainability awareness.
